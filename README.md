# Zomato-Restaurants-Analytics-Dashboard

## Overview
This project presents an interactive analytics dashboard built using Zomato restaurant data to analyze restaurant distribution, pricing patterns, customer ratings, cuisine diversity, and service availability across multiple countries and cities.

The dashboard enables exploratory analysis and high-level business insights into the global food and restaurant ecosystem represented on the Zomato platform.

---

## Objectives
- Analyze the global distribution of restaurants listed on Zomato  
- Compare average ratings across countries  
- Evaluate average cost for two across major cities  
- Assess adoption of online delivery and table booking services  
- Provide a consolidated, interactive view of restaurant analytics  

---

## Key Metrics
- **Total Restaurants:** 9,551  
- **Total Cuisines:** 1,826  
- **Countries Covered:** 15  
- **Average Cost for Two:** ₹1.20K  

---

## Insights Summary
### Ratings Analysis
- Higher average ratings are observed in countries such as the Philippines, Turkey, and Indonesia.
- India shows a comparatively lower average rating, indicating scope for service or quality improvement.

### Cost Analysis
- Cities including Panchkula, Hyderabad, Pune, and Jaipur exhibit higher average dining costs.
- Several cities maintain moderate pricing while offering competitive customer ratings.

### Service Availability
- **Online Delivery:** Supported by approximately 74% of restaurants.
- **Table Booking:** Available in about 12% of restaurants, indicating limited adoption.
